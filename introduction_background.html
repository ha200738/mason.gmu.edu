Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare

Huria Ahmad

Introduction & Background

Though artificial intelligence (AI) has the potential to change many industries, healthcare remains one of its most exciting applications. AI can process massive datasets, recognize patterns, and assist healthcare workers with decision-making through diagnostic systems and predictive analytics. There are advantages like AI in healthcare use cases, but disadvantages like security, social issues, or other legal and ethical issues as well. To fully utilize AI's potential, its complex effects on healthcare must be identified among individuals through empirical guidance.(Brynjolfsson & McAfee, 2014).

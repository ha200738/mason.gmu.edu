Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare

Huria Ahmad

Conclusion

The potential of AI in healthcare is enormous, but more research is urgently needed to address the technical, ethical, and legal challenges involved. Developing efficient and impartial methods is needed with consideration of the diversity of experimental data and scenarios of AI usage. (Obermeyer et al., 2019). Before AI can be integrated into healthcare safety and ethics, we have to ramp up cybersecurity to safeguard patient data along with legal regulations surrounding transparency of responsibility in the use of AI for medical decision-making. By transcending these barriers, the healthcare sector might move closer to fulfilling AI's critical promise and improving patient care and results.

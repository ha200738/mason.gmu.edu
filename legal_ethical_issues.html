Legal & Ethical Issues

There are significant moral and legal issues related to the use of AI in medicine. One of the primary challenges is patient privacy because artificial intelligence relies on massive datasets that must contain personal health data. Private information is protected through strict adherence to laws, such as the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) in the US. For example, when an AI-assisted medical decision leads to a poor outcome, who is responsible?

That is only one portion of the puzzle when trying to establish liability, as organizations, healthcare providers, and technology developers may have their share of blame. (Cabuyao, 2023). As stated by Barocas et al. (2019), the bias in artificial intelligence has raised many ethical issues as well.
